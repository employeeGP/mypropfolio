Your microsite stays live until the end of your hosting period. Once sold, you can let it expire or redirect it to your personal agent profile. Active listings on monthly plans continue until the end of the final paid month.
The per-listing price covers everything: design, build, AI location research, and 2 months of hosting. No separate setup fees or hidden charges. After 2 months, extend at $25/month or grab the 3-month bundle for $60 (saves $15). All property types, same rate.
  • Minimum 5 photos, maximum 10 photos per listing
  • Accepted formats: JPEG or PNG, minimum 1MB per file
  • Photos must be of the listed property — no stock or watermarked images
  • You confirm you have the rights to use all photos submitted
  • Floor plan: any quality accepted (photo, scan, or PDF)
  • Video tour: provide a YouTube link — raw video files not accepted
  • The clock starts only when we have your complete intake form, all photos, and full payment — not on partial submission
  • Standard delivery: 48 business hours | Rush: 24 business hours (+$150, subject to availability)

1 round of revisions included. All feedback must be submitted in one consolidated message.

Included:

  • Listing price update
  • Swap up to 3 photos (within original 10-photo limit)
  • Correct factual errors (address, size, room count, tenure)
  • Add or replace floor plan image
  • Add or update video tour link
  • Update agent contact details or profile photo

Chargeable at $60 per round:

  • More than 3 new photos after go-live
  • Layout or design style changes
  • Rewriting the AI location description from scratch
  • Structural content changes requiring significant rework

Revisions requested more than 30 days after go-live are treated as a new paid round.
