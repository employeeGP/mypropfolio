What is B1 Light Industrial?

B1 is a zoning classification by URA (Urban Redevelopment Authority) for clean, light industrial uses. It sits between commercial (B2 heavy industrial) and pure office — ideal for businesses that need both workspace and production capacity.

B1 units are popular with SMEs, F&B operators, creative agencies, e-commerce businesses, and traders who want the practicality of industrial space with the accessibility of a commercial address.

Psf pricing is consistently lower than commercial shophouses or office space for a similar floor area — making B1 one of the most cost-effective ways to own your business premises in Singapore.
