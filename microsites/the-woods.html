Understanding Your Purchase

What is
cluster landed?

The Woods is a strata landed development — a format that combines the privacy and space of a landed home with select condominium facilities. You own your unit and the strata lot outright. Unlike an HDB or standard condo, you have a physical address, your own car porch, and direct access to your home.

It's a different class of ownership compared to a private freehold landed, but for buyers who want the landed lifestyle without the full landed price tag, cluster developments like The Woods offer compelling value — especially with facilities and security management included.

What You Own
Your strata unit + strata lot. Individual address, own car porch, direct entry. No shared walls with units above or below.
Shared Facilities
Lap pool, gym, clubhouse, children's pool, koi pond, lantern pavilion — all maintained by the MCST. Monthly maintenance applies (~S$300–S$500).
Renovation Rules
Interior renovations are generally permitted. Exterior changes require MCST approval. No A&A works that affect the development's shared structure or façade.
Foreigners Can Buy
Strata landed in approved cluster developments is open to Singapore PRs and foreigners — unlike freehold private landed which requires SLA approval. ABSD applies.
