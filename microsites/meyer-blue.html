The Vision

Cabanas in
the Sky.

MEYER BLUE is conceived as a tropical modernist retreat — an urban resort where the sea and the city become your permanent backdrop. Set on the former Meyer Park site, it sits on 96,672 sqft of rare freehold land along one of Singapore's most coveted coastal addresses.

The architecture draws from resort living: cascading terraces, lush landscaping, and sky-high amenities that blur the boundary between indoors and out. Every unit is oriented to capture either sea views or the green landed enclave below.
