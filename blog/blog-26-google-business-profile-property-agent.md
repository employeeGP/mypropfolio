# How to Set Up Google Business Profile for Singapore Property Agents (2026)

When someone Googles "HDB agent Tampines" or "property agent Bukit Timah" or your own name, what comes up? For most Singapore property agents, the answer is a PropertyGuru profile — if anything at all.

A Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) changes this. It is free, takes under an hour to set up, and creates a professional card in Google search results and Google Maps that shows your photo, specialisation, contact details, reviews, and a direct WhatsApp link — independently of any portal.

<cite index="50-1">A well-built property agent website with Google Business Profile can begin generating visibility within weeks of setup — significantly faster than content SEO which takes 3-6 months.</cite>

Here is exactly how to set it up and optimise it for the Singapore property market.

---

## Why Google Business Profile Matters for Property Agents

Most Singapore buyers and sellers who are actively looking for an agent search Google — not just portals. Searches like:

- "[Your name] property agent" — direct searches by people who were referred to you
- "HDB agent [estate]" — buyers and sellers looking for a local specialist
- "property agent near me" — location-based searches from buyers researching their options
- "condo agent [district]" — segment-specific searches

Without a Google Business Profile, you appear nowhere in these searches. With one, you appear as a professional card in the local results — with your photo, reviews, specialisation, and a one-tap WhatsApp button.

<cite index="52-1">Use paid social media advertising to promote your highest-value listings to targeted audiences. But first, ensure your organic presence is complete — buyers who search for you after seeing your social content or portal listing expect to find something credible when they Google your name.</cite>

A Google Business Profile is that credibility signal.

---

## Step 1: Create Your Google Business Profile

**Go to:** business.google.com → click "Manage now" → sign in with your Google account → "Add your business"

**Business name:** Use your professional name as a property agent. Options:
- "[Your Name] — Property Agent Singapore" (simplest, most searchable)
- "[Your Name] — HDB Resale Specialist" (niche-signalling)
- "[Your Name] | PropNex / ERA / Huttons" (agency-branded)

Recommendation: Include your niche in the business name. "Sarah Tan — HDB Resale Specialist Tampines" ranks better for niche searches than "Sarah Tan PropNex."

**Category:** Select "Real Estate Agent" as your primary category. If you do both sales and rentals, you can add "Real Estate Rental Agency" as a secondary category.

**Location:** Property agents are service-area businesses — you serve clients across Singapore, not at a fixed address. Select "I deliver goods and services to my customers" and specify your service areas (districts or estates you work in).

**Phone number:** Use your primary WhatsApp number. Buyers expect to be able to WhatsApp directly from the listing.

**Website:** Link to your personal website if you have one, or to your mypropfolio agent profile page. This gives Google additional context about your business and gives profile visitors a destination to learn more.

---

## Step 2: Verify Your Business

Google will send a verification postcard to your registered address, or may offer phone/email verification for service-area businesses. Complete verification before optimising further — unverified profiles have limited visibility.

Verification typically takes 5-14 days for postcard delivery. Phone and email verification options (when offered) are immediate.

---

## Step 3: Complete Your Business Description

The description section (up to 750 characters) is your primary opportunity to tell Google and potential clients what you do. Include:

- Your niche and specialisation
- The areas you focus on
- Your years of experience (if relevant)
- Your CEA registration number
- A brief value proposition

**Example description:**

"HDB resale and upgrading specialist with 6 years of experience in Tampines, Punggol, and Sengkang. I help families navigate the HDB-to-condo upgrade journey with data-driven pricing, professional listing presentation, and clear communication throughout the process. CEA Reg: R[XXXXXX]. WhatsApp: +65 9XXX XXXX."

This description covers: niche → areas → experience → what you do for clients → compliance information → contact.

---

## Step 4: Add Professional Photos

Google Business Profiles with photos generate significantly more clicks than profiles without them.

**Required photos:**
- **Profile photo:** Your professional headshot — the same one you use across all platforms
- **Cover photo:** A banner image showing you with a property, a branded background, or a clean professional setting

**Additional photos to add:**
- Action shots from listing appointments or viewings (with client permission)
- Examples of your listing presentation or signboards
- Your recent transactions (transaction amounts can be included if accurate and compliant with CEA guidelines)

Update your photos when you change your headshot or when you want to refresh the profile's visual content.

---

## Step 5: Set Up Your Services

Add the specific services you offer as structured entries in the Services section:

- HDB Resale (if applicable)
- Private Property Sales
- Rental Properties
- Property Valuation
- Investment Properties

Including specific services helps Google match your profile to specific search queries beyond just "property agent."

---

## Step 6: Create Regular Google Posts

Google Business Profile allows you to publish posts that appear directly in your search panel — similar to social media updates but displayed in Google search results.

Post types that work well for property agents:

**Transaction updates (highest trust signal):**
"Just closed: 4-room HDB in Tampines at SGD 680K, 12 days on market. If you are considering selling in the area, I can share what buyers are looking for right now."

**Market updates:**
"Tampines HDB resale — June 2026: 4-room median at SGD 628K, 23 transactions recorded. Comment below if you want the full estate breakdown."

**New listings:**
"New listing: 5-room HDB, Tampines Street 21, SGD 720K. Professional photos, full floor plan, and WhatsApp CTA — [mypropfolio link]. Viewing available this weekend."

Post once per week minimum. Posts remain visible for 7 days before expiring unless you pin them.

---

## Step 7: Collect Reviews Actively

Reviews are the most powerful element of a Google Business Profile for conversion — more impactful than photos, services, or posts combined.

<cite index="52-1">Property buyers and sellers in Singapore research agents extensively online before engaging. They look for market expertise, transaction track record, and client testimonials.</cite>

A Google review with specific outcomes ("Sarah sold our Tampines 4-room in 8 days above asking — her market analysis was spot on") is significantly more persuasive than a generic "great agent" review.

**How to collect reviews:**

After every successful transaction, send your client a direct link to your Google review page:
`https://search.google.com/local/writereview?placeid=[your profile's place ID]`

Include this in your post-transaction follow-up: "If you found working together valuable, I would really appreciate a review — here is the direct link. A few sentences about your experience and the outcome of the transaction would mean a lot."

Target: 1-2 new reviews per month. After 10-15 reviews, your profile begins to rank significantly better for local searches.

---

## Step 8: Respond to All Reviews

Respond to every review — positive and negative — promptly and professionally.

For positive reviews: "Thank you [Name] — it was a pleasure helping you [outcome]. Wishing you all the best in your new home. Feel free to reach out for any future property needs."

For negative reviews (handle carefully): Acknowledge the experience, provide your perspective professionally, and offer to continue the conversation offline. Do not be defensive or dismissive — other potential clients are reading your response as much as the review itself.

---

## Integrating Google Business Profile With Your Other Channels

Google Business Profile works best as part of an integrated online presence:

**Social media:** When you post a transaction milestone on LinkedIn or Instagram, mention that you left a Google review request with the client. This normalises the review-requesting behaviour.

**mypropfolio listing links:** Include your mypropfolio listing page URL in your Google posts for new listings. This creates a consistent, professional experience from the Google search panel to the listing page.

**WhatsApp business:** Ensure the WhatsApp number on your Google Business Profile matches your WhatsApp Business account — consistency across platforms builds trust.
