# PropertyGuru Alternatives for Singapore Property Agents: What Actually Works in 2026

PropertyGuru is expensive. <cite index="43-1">A featured listing on PropertyGuru can cost SGD 200 to 500 per month with no guarantee of enquiries.</cite> And with <cite index="47-1">over 36,000 property agents in Singapore competing for the same leads on the same portals</cite>, the ROI on portal spend is declining year on year as competition intensifies.

This does not mean agents should abandon PropertyGuru — its 81-82% market share makes it functionally non-negotiable for discovery. But it does mean that agents who rely exclusively on PropertyGuru are paying more for less, while agents who have built lead sources beyond the portal have a structural advantage.

Here is what actually works as a complement to — or partial substitute for — PropertyGuru spend in Singapore's 2026 market.

---

## The Honest Context First

Let us be direct: no single tool replaces PropertyGuru for top-of-funnel property discovery in Singapore. The buyers are there. The search volume is there. Pulling out entirely would mean missing the majority of actively searching buyers.

What is worth examining is whether every dollar of portal spend is earning its return — and whether some of that budget could generate better ROI deployed elsewhere. For most agents, the answer is yes.

---

## Alternative 1: 99.co (Best Direct Portal Alternative)

**What it is:** Singapore's second-largest property portal, with strong map-based search and a growing user base.

**Why it works:** <cite index="33-1">99.co maintains its position as the UX leader, specifically for users who prioritise location. Their map-based search is not just a feature — it is the core of their interface.</cite>

For agents who are paying for featured placement on PropertyGuru for every listing, 99.co offers a credible secondary portal at lower cost. Buyers who find your listing on 99.co are often different from PropertyGuru users — slightly younger, more UX-driven, and often searching by location rather than by agent.

**Best for:** Agents who want secondary portal coverage, particularly for rental listings and HDB resale where 99.co has strong inventory.

**Limitation:** Smaller database means less total reach than PropertyGuru. Works best as a complement, not a replacement.

---

## Alternative 2: A Dedicated Listing Microsite (Best Conversion Alternative)

**What it is:** A branded page created for each individual listing — like those generated by mypropfolio — that gives buyers a professional, comprehensive experience outside the portal environment.

**Why it works differently from a portal:** On PropertyGuru, a buyer who clicks your listing sees your property competing with adjacent listings, surrounded by portal branding. On a mypropfolio page, the buyer sees only your property, your branding, and a direct WhatsApp button.

<cite index="43-1">On a portal, your listing is one of thousands on the same page, competing against agents who paid for premium placement. A property website helps keep your brand front and centre throughout the buyer journey.</cite>

**The practical advantage:** A mypropfolio link can be shared on WhatsApp, posted on Instagram, used in broadcast messages, and shown at listing appointments — functioning as a marketing asset across multiple channels simultaneously. A portal listing only works when a buyer is actively searching the portal.

For agents looking to reduce their portal spend without losing listing quality, mypropfolio listing pages allow you to maintain a professional, comprehensive listing presence across all direct-sharing channels — at a fraction of the cost of featured portal placement.

**Best for:** Every agent who wants to convert portal discovery into committed enquiries, and agents who want to reduce dependency on expensive portal featured placements.

---

## Alternative 3: Google Business Profile (Best Free Local Alternative)

**What it is:** A free business listing on Google that appears when someone searches your name or "property agent [district]."

**Why it works:** <cite index="47-1">An SEO real estate agent Singapore strategy generates enquiries that come directly to you — no listing fees per property, no competing with thirty other agents on the same page, and traffic that compounds over time instead of resetting to zero the moment your subscription lapses.</cite>

A well-optimised Google Business Profile puts you on the map — literally — for local searches. When a seller searches "HDB agent Tampines" or "property agent Bishan", your profile can appear in the local results panel with your photo, specialisation, reviews, and a direct WhatsApp link.

Setup takes 30 minutes. Maintenance is minimal. And unlike portal spend, the visibility compounds over time as you accumulate reviews.

**Best for:** Every agent — this is the highest-ROI free tool available. The marginal cost is zero; the only investment is time.

**Limitation:** Does not provide the listing inventory discovery that PropertyGuru offers. Works for agent discovery searches, not property-specific searches.

---

## Alternative 4: Social Media Video (Best Organic Lead Alternative)

**What it is:** Short-form video content on TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube that showcases listings, market commentary, and neighbourhood guides.

**Why it works:** <cite index="33-1">A single TikTok video can reach 10,000 to 50,000 potential buyers organically — at zero media cost.</cite> <cite index="43-1">Agents posting 3 to 5 videos per week typically generate 10 to 30 qualified enquiries monthly from organic social media.</cite>

The cost per lead from organic video is dramatically lower than PropertyGuru featured placement. The trade-off is time: video content requires consistent production, and results build over 3-6 months rather than immediately.

**Best for:** Agents with a 6-12 month horizon who want to build a lead pipeline that does not reset to zero when they stop paying. Particularly effective for agents targeting younger HDB upgrader buyers on TikTok or Instagram.

**Limitation:** Does not replace portal reach for buyers who are actively searching listings. Works as a top-of-funnel awareness channel, not a bottom-of-funnel listing discovery tool.

---

## Alternative 5: LinkedIn (Best for Investor and Corporate Segments)

**What it is:** Professional network with 3.2 million Singapore users, heavily concentrated in the professional and corporate segments.

**Why it works as an alternative:** <cite index="49-1">LinkedIn reaches Singapore professionals including high-net-worth buyers, corporate relocation clients, and investor audiences. LinkedIn gives agents access to executives, expats, and business owners with higher purchasing power than audiences on TikTok or Instagram. Fewer than 5% of Singapore property agents post actively on LinkedIn.</cite>

For agents who specialise in the private property segment — particularly condos, landed homes, and investment properties — LinkedIn reaches a segment that is underserved by portal advertising and almost completely untouched by other agents.

**Best for:** Agents targeting HDB upgraders, investors, or corporate expat tenants. Less relevant for agents focused primarily on HDB resale within the mass market.

**Limitation:** Does not provide listing inventory discovery. Works for agent discovery and relationship building, not for buyers actively searching for specific properties.

## How to Reduce PropertyGuru Dependency Strategically

Rather than cutting portal spend abruptly, the most effective approach is a gradual rebalancing:

**Month 1-2:** Set up Google Business Profile, create WhatsApp Business profile, start mypropfolio for all new listings. Zero additional cost.

**Month 3-4:** Begin posting 2-3 times per week on one social platform (Instagram or LinkedIn depending on your target segment). Track which channel generates inbound contact.

**Month 5-6:** Review portal spend against lead quality. If mypropfolio + social + Google is generating comparable lead quality to featured portal placement, consider reducing featured listing spend on lower-value listings.

**Month 6+:** Maintain PropertyGuru presence for discovery, use mypropfolio for all conversion touchpoints, and let social media compound into an organic lead pipeline over time.

This approach reduces portal dependency without abandoning the reach that still generates the majority of Singapore buyer discovery.

---

## The Bottom Line

PropertyGuru is not going away, and pulling out entirely would be a strategic mistake for most Singapore agents. But treating it as the only tool — and paying escalating fees for the privilege of competing with 36,000 other agents on the same platform — is equally untenable.

The agents building sustainable lead pipelines in 2026 are using PropertyGuru for what it does best (reach and discovery), mypropfolio for what portal listings cannot do (conversion, branding, and professional sharing), Google Business Profile and LinkedIn for organic visibility, and social media video for long-term pipeline building.

None of these alternatives replace PropertyGuru. All of them reduce your dependency on it.
